üß† Common Hacking Methods

Phishing: Fake emails or texts to steal credentials.

Smishing: SMS-based phishing with fake links.

Malware & Spyware: Steals data via malicious apps.

SIM Swapping: Hijacks phone numbers for account access.

Public Wi-Fi Attacks: Intercepts unsecured data transfers.

Evil Twin Networks: Fake Wi-Fi mimicking real hotspots.

Bluetooth Hacking: Exploits active Bluetooth connections.

Fake Apps: Trojans disguised as trusted software.

Juice Jacking: Malware via public USB ports.

Rogue QR Codes: Redirects to malicious pages.

Social Engineering: Manipulation to reveal secrets.

Credential Stuffing: Uses breached passwords elsewhere.

Man-in-the-Middle: Data interception between endpoints.

Fake Support Scams: Impersonation of legit services.

Social Media/Discord Hacking: Token theft and phishing scams.

üìò Real-World Examples

  • Wi-Fi Interception: Hacker-controlled network steals email login.
  • Discord Nitro Scam: Token stolen after login to fake giveaway page.
