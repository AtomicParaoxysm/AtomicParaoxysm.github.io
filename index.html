Introduction

Why cybersecurity is important and how hackers exploit vulnerabilities.

Common Hacking Methods

  • Phishing: Fake emails, texts, or sites trick users into revealing data.
  • Malware: Harmful apps infect devices and steal information.
  • SIM Swapping: Attackers hijack your phone number.
  • Public Wi-Fi Attacks: Hackers intercept data on unsecured networks.

Real-World Hacking Examples

Phishing Scam: Fake banking websites steal login credentials.

Malware Attack: Malicious apps secretly install spyware.

Public Wi-Fi Interception: Hackers steal data from open networks.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Use strong passwords & enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Keep software updated & install security patches.
  • Avoid clicking suspicious links or downloading unknown apps.
  • Use a VPN when connecting to public Wi-Fi.

Protecting Your Discord Account

Common hacking methods: Token theft, phishing, and credential stuffing.

Safety Tips: Enable 2FA, use strong passwords, and avoid clicking unknown links.
